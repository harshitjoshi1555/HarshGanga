About Us

Prathavi Raj Joshi is a hardworking and dedicated individual who started a small shop in 2002 and has since expanded his business to become a respected member of his community. With a passion for entrepreneurship and a commitment to providing excellent customer service, Prathavi has built a successful business that has stood the test of time. In addition to his entrepreneurial pursuits, Prathavi is also actively involved in his local government. He has worked as a CSC (Common Service Center) operator, providing valuable services to his fellow citizens, and as a BLO (Block Level Officer), helping to ensure that his community's needs are being met.

Prathavi's dedication to his work is evident in his accomplishments. He has received numerous accolades for his contributions to his community, including awards for his outstanding service as a CSC operator and BLO. Despite his busy schedule, Prathavi always makes time for his family and friends. He is a devoted husband and father who enjoys spending time with his loved ones and engaging in activities that bring him joy. Overall, Prathavi Raj Joshi is a driven and passionate individual who has made significant contributions to his community. His commitment to entrepreneurship and public service make him a valuable member of any team or organization.

What We Do

Our business is a CSC (Common Service Center) located in Chaukhutia, where we offer a wide range of government-related services to the local community. Our services include document printing and scanning, Aadhaar card services, PAN card services, and various other government-related tasks. Our business is essential to the community as it provides a convenient and accessible option for citizens to complete their government-related work. By offering these services in one location, we save our customers time and effort that they would otherwise have to spend traveling to different government offices.
